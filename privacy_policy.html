Privacy Policy for Morsel

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

At Morsel, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application ("Morsel").

1. Information We Collect

To provide a synchronized experience across devices and enable personal recipe management, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, password (stored securely via Firebase), and username.
  • User-Generated Content: Recipes you create, edit, or customize, including ingredients, steps, names, and images you link or capture, are stored in our secure database.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous diagnostic and usage data (such as app logs and crash reports) to improve application performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and allow you to log in to your account.
  • To synchronize your recipes and settings across multiple devices using Google Firebase.
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • To monitor, analyze, and improve the functionality of the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Morsel integrates third-party services to handle authentication, database storage, and diagnostics. By using the app, you agree to their privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore): Used for secure user accounts, data storage, and syncing. Under the hood, Google collects temporary connection metadata and diagnostic identifiers.
  • Google Sign-In / Credential Manager: Used to facilitate quick and secure authentication.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your custom recipes at any time within the app.
  • You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated personal data by contacting us at the email address listed below. Upon request, we will purge all data from Firebase.

5. Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, utilizing Firebase's industry-standard database encryption (both in transit and at rest). However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Morsel is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
