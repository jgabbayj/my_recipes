Pre-Alpha Version (1.0) - Initial Internal Testing

Key Features in this build:
- **Recipe Dashboard**: Browse, filter, and organize your favorite recipes.
- **Smart Recipe Form**: Add and edit recipes, powered by Gemini-based parsing for ingredients and steps.
- **Interactive Cooking Mode**: Step-by-step guidance designed to make cooking easy and hands-free.
- **Secure Authentication**: Built-in Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In support.
- **Cloud Database**: Real-time recipe syncing powered by Firestore.

Note: This is an early pre-alpha build for internal testing. Some UI elements, integrations, and functions are under active development and subject to change. Please report any bugs or feedback to the development team.
